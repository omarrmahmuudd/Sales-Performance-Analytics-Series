## 📊 Business Case & Overview
In today’s data-driven world, a single view isn't always enough. This project showcases a comprehensive sales analysis built to help stakeholders monitor business health across different regions and product lines. By using multiple visual themes, I demonstrate how data can be tailored to match any corporate identity while maintaining high functional standards.

## ❓ Business Questions Addressed
This series of dashboards was designed to answer critical business questions:
1. **Revenue Trends:** What is the monthly sales growth, and are we hitting our targets?
2. **Product Strategy:** Which product categories contribute most to the total profit?
3. **Customer Insights:** How are sales distributed across different segments?
4. **Regional Performance:** Which geographical areas require more marketing focus?

## 🛠️ Technical Workflow (End-to-End)
To ensure data accuracy and visual impact, I followed these steps:
* **Data Cleaning (Power Query):** Handled missing values, adjusted data types, and created custom columns for fiscal periods.
* **Data Modeling:** Established a "Star Schema" for efficient performance and easier filtering.
* **DAX Calculations:** Built custom measures for Total Sales, YTD Profit, and Variance % between years.
* **UI/UX Design:** Iterated through 6+ different visual themes to find the best balance between aesthetics and readability.
